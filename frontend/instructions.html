Test Instructions

Read carefully before you begin. The test has 2 sections and is auto-timed.

SECTION 1 • WRITTEN

30 minutes

  • 15 multiple-choice questions covering grammar, reading comprehension, and vocabulary.
  • One reading passage on the left; questions about it on the right.
  • Other questions stand alone (grammar, fill-in-blank, synonyms).
  • The timer is visible at the top-right of the screen.
SECTION 2 • SPEAKING

up to 10 minutes

  • 3 impromptu speaking prompts.
  • Speak for ~60–90 seconds per topic.
  • Speak clearly into your microphone.
  • You cannot re-record once submitted.
Important: Your scores will not be shown after submission. Results are emailed directly to the HR manager. Do not refresh or close the browser during the test — the timer keeps running. Make sure your microphone is enabled (a browser permission prompt will appear when you start the speaking section).
