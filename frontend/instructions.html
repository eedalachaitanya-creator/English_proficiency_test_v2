Test Instructions

Read carefully before you begin. The test has 3 sections and is auto-timed.

SECTION 1 • READING

30 minutes

  • 15 multiple-choice questions.
  • Mix of grammar, reading comprehension, vocabulary, fill-in-the-blank.
  • One reading passage with questions about it.
  • Timer visible at the top-right.
SECTION 2 • WRITING

20 minutes

  • One essay prompt on a real-world topic.
  • Word range shown on screen (200–300 or 300–450 by level).
  • Live word counter as you type.
  • Auto-saves every keystroke.
SECTION 3 • SPEAKING

up to 10 minutes

  • 3 impromptu speaking prompts.
  • Speak for ~60–90 seconds per topic.
  • Speak clearly into your microphone.
  • You cannot re-record once submitted.
Important: Your scores will not be shown after submission. Results are emailed directly to the HR manager. Do not refresh or close the browser during the test — the timer keeps running. Make sure your microphone is enabled (a browser permission prompt will appear when you start the speaking section).
